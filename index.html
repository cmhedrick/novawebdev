What We do

We offer some of the newest technology for building the ideal website. We use HTML5 which is the newest markup language availble to construct the site. To style the site we use CSS3 to help with styling and creating a responsive design. We also use Javascript and Jquery to make the site interactive. Our backend of choice is also the ever popular Django to tie everything together!

About

NOVA Web Development provides web page design and web application development at affordable rates for civic, academic, non-profit, and small business enterprises. Our clients include Buyers and Renters Arlington Voice (BRAVO), Gallaudet University, and the Rosslyn Chamber of Commerce.

Service

We offer web page design and web application development using the latest open web standards and free software tools. We speak HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Python, and the Django web framework. Our websites are responsive and we have experience with data visualization.

Who We Are

We are a co-op with an educational and service focus. Our young web professionals work side-by-side with experienced veterans learning state-of-the-art webcraft providing solutions to problems facing the local community. We embrace a social justice mission with a dedication to software freedom and strive to create the world we want to live in.
